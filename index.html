The Open Source Energy Modelling System ({{ site.tool_name }})

OSeMOSYS is an open source modelling system for long-term integrated assessment and energy planning. It has been employed to develop energy systems models from the scale of the globe, continents, countries, regions and villages. It can focus on detailed power representations, or multi-resource (material, financial, all energy) systems.
